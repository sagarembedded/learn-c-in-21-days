### **Exercise 1 ‚Äì Print Your Name and Age**

- Write a program to print your **name** and **age** in separate lines.

**Example Output:**

* Age: 22

- Modify the `addition.c` example to also calculate **difference, product, and quotient**.  
- Take two integers as input and display all four results.

**Sample Output:**

### **Exercise 3 ‚Äì Circle Calculations**

- Expand `variables_constants.c` to calculate **area** and **circumference** of a circle.

### **Exercise 5 ‚Äì Using Multiple Variables**
